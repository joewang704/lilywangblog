About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Lily Wang, and I am an aspiring optometrist as well as a Microbiology student at the University of Georgia. I got my first DSLR camera in high school and fell in love with photography. I bring my camera everywhere I go and take pictures of about anything that captures my attention. Need a picture? I got you.

Photo Blog

I can now cross off going on an Alaskan cruise off my bucket list! Despite having to deal with cold temperatures in the summer, the trip was worth it. I was able to explore Alaskan forests, villages, and even get to see a bear or two. It was an amazing experience, and I hope to go again in the future.
This picture was taken on my trip to Hilton Head. I normally wake up after 10 AM when school is not in session, but on this morning, I was dragged out of bed by my mother to watch the sun rise. Even though I was groggy and wanted go back to sleep, I’m glad I was able to take this picture. And the couple just happened to be conveniently there.
Meet Sharon! She absolutely hates having her pictures taken. Lucky for her, I was there. The weather was nice and sunny outside, so I decided to have an impromptu photo shoot! Even with just a t­shirt and jacket on, she still looked pretty awesome.
He’s clever, warm, loves playing with string, a bit chubby, and absolutely adorable. Here’s Bobo! He usually spends the day sleeping away either on my bed or on the couch, but when he’s not, he likes to explore different places of the house; he can even open doors. He also loves to cuddle, and if he doesn’t have your attention, he will jump on you and force you to cuddle with him.
This picture was taken in Madison, GA. There’s nothing quite like the feeling of walking through a small town at night during the holidays. I took this picture at about 8 pm, and there were barely any cars on the streets and almost no one walking about. Having lived in the Atlanta area for most of my life, I’ve never seen a city so quiet so early.
